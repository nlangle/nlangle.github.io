Full Service Auto Repair in Lakewood

Lube & Latte has provided professional automotive repair services to the community of Lakewood for over 10 years. We believe in providing you with an honest and unique automotive repair experience. Whether the job calls for an oil change or a complete overhaul, our goal is earning your trust and creating a long lasting relationship. Our friendly team has the experience, expertise (and caffeine!) to keep you and your vehicle going.

Fresh

Get ready for a different type of automotive repair experience! You're sure to feel welcomed by the inviting atmosphere when you step foot into our cafe. If you're waiting while your vehicle is being serviced by our ASE qualified staff, we invite you to get comfortable and relax. Reputable

All of our technicians are ASE certified, so you can feel confident that your vehicle is in good hands in our shop. Lube & Latte has a sterling reputation and has received awards and recognition for excellent quality of customer service.
