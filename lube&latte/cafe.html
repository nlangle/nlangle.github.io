Welcome to our Cafe

Whether you're hanging out while your vehicle is serviced, or just stopping by to grab something fresh from our menu, we want you to feel at home.

Try one of our hand crafted cafe drinks, fresh brewed with local sourced coffee beans. Our drink menu features both hot and iced coffee drinks, hot chocolate and smoothies. If you need a bite to eat, enjoy pastries and muffins from local bakers.

Guests enjoy free wi-fi. All premium oil change services include one complimentary beverage.
