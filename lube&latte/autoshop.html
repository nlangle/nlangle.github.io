Automotive Repair Services in Lakewood

At Lube & Latte we believe in providing the best service possible for you and your vehicle. Our team of ASE certified technicians work on all makes and models. Our shop provides a variety of services, from full service repair, scheduled maintenance, unexpected repairs, diagnostics and, of course, lube and oil changes.

Lube & Latte is a customer first repair shop, and we believe in providing a refreshing automotive repair experience. We want you to feel welcome, that is why we strive to be the cleanest and most presentable repair shop in the area and why we have a cafe where you can treat yourself when you stop by.


We stand behind our work, and offer a 1 year / 12,000 mile labor warranty on most repairs.


We believe in convenience. If you're having a large repair performed that will take longer than you can wait, feel free to ask about our courtesy vehicle. We also offer an after hours key drop, so you can drop your vehicle off if you can't make it during our normal hours of operation.


If your vehicle's check engine light is on or you notice puddles of fluid beneath your parking spot, don't hesitate to have a professional inspect your vehicle right away to make sure any potential problems don't cause further damage!
