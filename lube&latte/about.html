About Us

Automotive Repair Services in Lakewood

Our goal is to provide a comfortable, honest and unique experience that consistently exceeds your expectations.

Lube & Latte is a family owned and operated repair shop conveniently located in Lakewood, Colorado. Our shop provides complete automotive repair services offers an inviting cafe where our customers can relax and wait or grab a drink to go when you drop your vehicle off for repair. We believe in providing comfort and added convenience for our customers, such as courtesy vehicles, after hours key drop and special towing rates if you use our towing partner.


We understand that you have many choices when it comes to selecting a shop to work on your car, and that is why we are proud to be recognized by the community as one of the top automotive repair locations in Lakewood. Our shop has been recognized by local publications as one of the top shops in town. We have also been featured in articles by national publications such as Ratchet+Wrench and Undercar Digest Magazine. We thank our customers for the continued business and support.
