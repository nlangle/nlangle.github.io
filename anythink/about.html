About Riverdale Road


Riverdale Road is an 11-mile winding stretch of road between Thornton and Brighton, and is, according to the local legend, very haunted. Our caffeinated coders have collected some of those spooky tales to share with you. Check out the Spooky Locations in the navigation of this site.

This story told by: Nick Langle on 10/25/2018
